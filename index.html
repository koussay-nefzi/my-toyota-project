Toyota Motor Corporation

- Toyota Motor Corporation, most commonly known as Toyota, is a multinational automotive manufacturer with headquarters in Toyota, Aichi, Japan. Kiichiro Toyoda founded and incorporated Toyota on August 28, 1937 . 364,445 employees from all around the globe made up Toyota’s corporate structure in 2017. Two years later, Toyota was the 10th largest company globally by revenue and one of the largest automobile manufacturers worldwide, based on sales. It actually managed to be the first one to produce over 10 million vehicles per year and has been doing so since 2012 . During the same year, Toyota announced the production of its 200 millionth vehicle, while in July 2014 , Toyota was the largest listed Japanese company based on market capitalisation and revenue.


- Toyota is undeniably a global market leader in selling hybrid electric vehicles, and hydrogen fuel-cell vehicles. In January 2020 , it raised 15 million through cumulative global sales of Toyota and Lexus hybrid passenger car models. Moreover, as of January 2017, Toyota Prius is the world’s top-selling hybrid vehicle with more than 6 million units sold worldwide.
